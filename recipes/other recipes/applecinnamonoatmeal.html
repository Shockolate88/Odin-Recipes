Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

This apple cinnamon oatmeal is so delicious! Full of fiber, protein, and warm spices paired with a subtle sweetness from honey, this healthy oatmeal breakfast recipe is the perfect option for anyone looking to add a little more nutrition and ease to their mornings.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Rolled Oats
  • Salt
  • Spices, Cinnamon and Nutmeg
  • Apples, peeled and chopped
  • Honey
  • pecans

Directions

  1. Boil the water: First, add water to a medium pot and bring to a boil. Add in the oats with salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Stir well.
  2. Next, add the chopped apples. Reduce the heat and simmer for ten to twelve minutes while occasionally stirring.
  3. Add Sweetner, once the apples are tender add in the honey and pecans
  4. Turn off the heat and cover the pot, let the oatmeal sit for 5 minutes

Recipe notes and tips

  1. Feel free to use maple syrup in place of honey for a vegan option.
  2. Store the leftovers in the refrigerator in an airtight container for four to six days.
  3. Use any type of apple for this apple cinnamon oatmeal recipe. We like Granny Smith, Gala, Honeycrisp, etc.
  4. To avoid gluten, make sure the oats are gluten-free. We have not tested instant oats.
  5. For even creamier options, stir in your choice of milk.
